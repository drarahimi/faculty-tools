UWindsor Committee Formation Rules

  • Ph.D. Dissertation: Min 4 members. Supervisor (Internal), 2 Program Readers (Internal), 1 Outside Program Reader (External).
  • Master's Thesis: Min 3 members. Supervisor (Internal), 2 Readers (Internal or External).
  • Master's Major Paper: Min 2 members. Supervisor (Internal), 1 Program Reader (Internal).

* The algorithm filters candidates internally/externally based on the selected Student Department and scores them against topic keywords.
