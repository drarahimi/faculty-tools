Mandatory International Travel Risk Protocol (Updated Nov 2025)

All international travel must be registered with Campus Safety prior to departure. Any academic travel to high-risk zones ("Avoid Non-Essential Travel") requires formal approval from the corresponding Vice-President or Provost.

Pre-Travel Authorization

Finance Policy Sec. 3

"One-Up" Rule

All travel must be authorized in writing/system by the person to whom you report (e.g. Department Head for Faculty, Dean for Heads) prior to booking.

Absence from Duties

Faculty members are responsible for arranging appropriate coverage of their teaching, exams, student consultations, or administrative duties, subject to Head/Dean approval.

Fund Verification

Ensure sufficient balance is available in your Professional Development (PD) account or specific Research Grant prior to booking. Over-expenditures require secondary approval.

Rules & Considerations

  • Meals Provided: You cannot claim per diem for meals that are already provided by a conference, workshop, flight, or hotel stay.
  • Alcohol Exclusion: Alcohol is strictly non-reimbursable. If ordering meals based on actual cost instead of per diems, ensure any alcohol is charged on a separate receipt. Refer to Finance Policy Sec. 6.1 .
  • Business Hosting: If hosting a guest or business dinner, you must claim actual meal costs using an itemized receipt (names and organizational affiliations of attendees required). You cannot claim per diems for hosting.

Transportation Policies

WUFA Mileage Rate
Per KM $0.55

Personal Car Allowance

Reimbursed for authorized driving. Limited to the cost of equivalent economy flight/rail if driving long-distance (e.g. Windsor to Ottawa).

Insurance (Ontario): Use corporate card where possible. Purchasing rental CDW inside Ontario is generally optional.
Insurance (Outside Ontario): Mandatory to purchase agency's Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) for all rental vehicles out-of-province/country. See Legal Services Rules .
Combined Personal Travel: If extending travel with vacation, you must print a quote of the business-only itinerary on the same day you book to establish the reimbursable cap.

Accommodations

Lodging Rules
Per Night $30.00

Gratuitous Lodging Allowance

Claimable without receipts if staying privately with friends or family instead of a commercial hotel. Encourages cost savings.

Standard Commercial Room: Reimbursement is limited to a single standard room rate. Luxury rooms/suites require VP approval.
Short-Term Rentals (Airbnb): Allowed if equivalent or cheaper than local standard commercial hotels. Final invoice/receipt is mandatory.

Receipt Standards & Timelines

Submission Rules

UWinsite Submission: Claims must be submitted via UWinsite Finance Expenses module within 15 working days of return.

Itemized Requirement: Booking receipts must show individual itemization (e.g. specific flight, ticket details, itemized foods). Credit card transaction slips with just the total are not acceptable.

Research Grants (Tri-Agency): Stricter guidelines apply. Original boarding passes (physical or digital screenshots) are mandatory to prove travel occurred.

Valid Hotel folio showing zero balance, itemized meal invoices, plane boarding passes.
Invalid Credit card purchase slips, booking confirmation emails without final invoice, credit card statements.
