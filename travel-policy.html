Pre-Travel Approval

Ref: Finance Policy Sec 3

"One-Up" Approval

All travel must be authorized in advance by the person to whom you report (e.g., Department Head for Faculty, Dean for Heads). This is mandatory prior to booking.

Absence from Duties

Faculty must ensure appropriate coverage for teaching, grading, or administrative duties while away from campus, subject to Head/Dean approval.

Funding Verification

Ensure sufficient Professional Development (PD) funds or specific grant accounts are available prior to incurring expenses. Over-expenditures require secondary approval.

Additional Provisions & Exceptions

  • Alcohol: Strictly not reimbursable under Travel Policy. Must be on a separate receipt. Exception: Pre-approved "Hospitality" events (requires Dean/VP approval).
  • Hosting Guests: If you pay for a guest's meal, you cannot use Per Diem. You must submit the itemized receipt for the whole table and claim it as "Entertainment" (names/affiliations required).
  • Currency: Rates are in CAD. For USA/International, you may claim the actual cost converted to CAD, or agree with your Dean on a USD equivalent prior to travel.

  • Economy Cap: If driving a long distance (e.g., to Ottawa), reimbursement is limited to the cost of the equivalent economy airfare/train ticket.
  • Proof: Attach a Google Maps screenshot showing start/end points and total kilometers.
  • Carpooling: Only the driver may claim mileage. Passengers cannot claim transport costs.

Rentals & Insurance

  • Insurance Rule (Ontario): Not strictly required if using UWindsor Corporate Card (check coverage).
  • Insurance Rule (Outside ON): Legal Services mandates purchasing the Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) from the rental agency for all rentals outside Ontario.
  • Gas: Claim actual gas receipts for rental cars (do not claim mileage).

Receipt Standards

Ref: Finance Policy

Valid Documentation

  • Itemized Receipts: Must show specific items ordered (e.g., "Burger", "Coffee"), not just the total.
  • Zero Balance: Hotel bills must show the payment was made and balance is $0.00.
  • Boarding Passes: Mandatory for all flights. Digital screenshots are accepted.
  • Exchange Rate: Credit card statement showing the CAD amount charged.

Common Rejections

  • Credit Card Slips: The small slip with just the total is not a valid receipt.
  • Booking Confirmations: An email saying "You are booked" is not a receipt. You need the final invoice.
  • Lost Receipts: If lost, you must complete a "Missing Receipt Affidavit" (Finance Form). Repeated use is monitored/flagged.

Special Scenarios & Deadlines

Claims & Advances

  • Submission Deadline: Claims should be submitted in UWinsite within 15 working days of returning.
  • Travel Advances: You can request a cash advance prior to the trip for major out-of-pocket costs (like international flights) to avoid carrying the financial burden.

Accommodations

  • Private Stays: If staying with friends or family, you may claim a gratuitous lodging allowance (no receipt needed, typically around $30/night).
  • Airbnb/Short-Term Rentals: Permitted if the cost is demonstrably equal to or less than a standard commercial hotel in the area.

Complex Travel Rules

  • Combined Personal Travel: If extending a business trip for vacation, you must obtain a quote for the business-only flight at the exact time of booking. You will only be reimbursed up to the cost of the business-only itinerary.
  • Spousal/Companion Travel: Travel costs for companions are strictly not reimbursable unless they have a pre-approved, documented University business purpose.
  • Visas & Immunizations: Required travel documents and mandatory health immunizations specifically required for the destination are generally reimbursable.
