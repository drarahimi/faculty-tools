Data Processing Workflow

Step 1: Cleanup

Detects assignment metadata (MaxPoints, Weight). Strips "Points Grade" suffix and everything after "<" to provide clean names like "Test 1" instead of full system strings.

Step 2: Conversion

Creates new columns adjacent to selections. Calculates Percent Equivalent using ROUNDUP((Score/Max)*100, 0) for every student entry.

Step 3: Accreditation

Appends CEAB-compliant statistics (DNME, Marginal, ME, EE) at the bottom using live Excel formulas. Applies Navy/White and Yellow branding with auto-fitted columns.
